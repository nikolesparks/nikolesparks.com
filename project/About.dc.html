My story

Therapy is its own creative process.

I'm an Associate Marriage and Family Therapist specializing in depth-oriented, attachment-based therapy in Orange County. I come to therapy by way of a career as a designer — 15+ years spent helping people uncover their unique point of view and expression. It's made me a firm believer that therapy is its own creative process: an invitation to slow down, listen deeply to what has shaped you, and make space for what wants to emerge.

My practice is grounded in psychodynamic therapy, an evidence-based approach that helps us explore the emotional and relational patterns that shape and guide our lives. Together, we'll trace these patterns with care — honoring where they come from while creating space for new possibilities.

I know firsthand that therapy can be life-changing. My own experience in therapy has shaped not only the way I practice, but the way I understand healing. I deeply respect the courage it takes to share your struggles with another person, and I value building a strong, collaborative relationship at your pace.

I typically work with people interested in longer-term (6+ months), in-depth therapy — people who want to meaningfully understand their inner emotional life, why they feel stuck, or why certain feelings and relational patterns keep recurring. I particularly enjoy working with creative professionals, those who struggle with self-expression, and those navigating the intersection of mental health and spirituality — whether integrating faith into the healing process or untangling pain connected to a spiritual background.
