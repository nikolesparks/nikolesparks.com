The approach

The client is the relationship.

My approach draws on attachment principles, helping you understand how your individual histories influence your current relationship patterns. Rather than taking sides, I work with the couple as a unit. Together, we'll explore the unconscious patterns creating disconnection, develop more satisfying ways to connect, and build a stronger foundation for intimacy and understanding.

The process

How the work unfolds

01
Creating safety

Our initial sessions establish a space where both partners feel safe to express themselves honestly. We identify the patterns in your relationship and what's happening beneath the surface of your conflicts.

02
Developing understanding

You'll gain insight into how your individual histories and attachment styles influence your relationship. This understanding often brings compassion where there was previously frustration.

03
Cultivating emotional presence

Partners learn to attune to each other's needs with greater sensitivity and responsiveness — a foundation where both feel seen and held, allowing for greater vulnerability and connection.

04
Deepening connection

As we address the patterns that create distance, many couples find their bond strengthened — a relationship spacious enough to hold the needs of two separate people choosing to journey together.
