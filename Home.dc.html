The approach

We won’t just manage the problem. We’ll get curious about where it came from.

Depth work means slowing down and asking why. Where do these patterns come from? What are you living out that you never consciously chose? The understanding you build here isn’t a tool you set aside after a few months — it becomes part of how you know yourself.
