My approach — psychodynamic therapy

Taking a depth approach to therapy.

Instead of advice, homework, or "just try this," psychodynamic therapy offers something deeper — a chance to understand what's actually going on beneath the surface of your creative, emotional life. The things that happened earlier in life, especially the ones we're not fully aware of, influence how we think, feel, and act now. The goal is to notice those patterns, understand how they're playing out, and free you up to live in a creative, generative way that reflects your truest self.
